Project Motivation


Retail traders often faces problem while making investment decisions due to many factors. like: lack of experience and knowledge, emotional biases, insufficient risk management, market volatility, uncertainity and so on.

Due to these problem, it is often cited that a large percentage of retail traders, ranging from 70% to 90%, end up losing money or are not consistently profitable

This project aims to leverage the power of deep learning, specificially Convolutional Neural Networks and Long Short-Term Memory (CNN-LSTM) model, to predict the closing stock prices for next trading day based on time series data. Additionally, the project integrates technical analysis-based recommendations to guide investors while making buy, hold, or sell decisions.

next >>>

Objective

1. Develop deep learnign (CNN-LSTM) model to predict stock prices for the next trading day based on time series data.

2. Incorporate technical analysis indicators to generate buy, hold, or sell recommendations.

3. Provide market related information in the same platform.
